About

Dr. Joffe is a general dentist. She treats children, adults, and seniors. Her priority is the health of your teeth and gums. She looks for tooth decay, gum disease, periodontal disease, oral cancer, and TMJ. She has over 25 years of personal experience and also works very closely with specialists, including an endodontist, a periodontist, and an orthodontist. She is a firm believer in conservative and preventative dentistry.

Dr. Helen Joffe

Dr. Joffe first practiced dentistry in St. Petersburg, Russia. In 1989 she moved to Chicago with her family and in order to continue to practice in the US, she again went through dental school at New York Dental School to earn her DDS. She currently is on staff at Great Lakes Naval Training Center in addition to having her own office. She continues to remain at the forefront of her profession by attending dental conventions and seminars. She is a member of the American Dental Association and the Illinois State Dental Society.
